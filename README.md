# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis (Tableau)

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn patterns in a subscription-based business using Tableau.

The goal is to identify:
- Why customers churn
- Which segments are at risk
- Key retention drivers
- Customer lifetime trends

---

##  Dashboard Features

- **Churn KPI** – Overall churn percentage
- **Churn Distribution** – % of churned vs retained customers
- **Churn by Contract** – Impact of contract type on churn
- **Churn by Tenure** – When customers are most likely to churn
- **Churn by Services** – Effect of services on retention

---

##  Key Insights

- High churn in **month-to-month contracts**
- Most churn occurs in the **first 6 months**
- Customers without **tech support & security services** have higher churn
- Long-term contracts significantly improve retention

---

##  Recommendations

- Encourage long-term contracts with incentives
- Improve onboarding experience
- Promote value-added services
- Target high-risk customers with retention strategies
